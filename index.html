FAQ

Questions patients ask before booking.

Do I need to know exactly which treatment I want?

No. The page is designed to push consultation bookings because many patients know the result they want, but not the exact treatment path.

Can this page be connected to WhatsApp?

Yes. Once the clinic provides a phone number, the CTA and form can open a pre-filled WhatsApp booking message.

Should we use real clinic photos?

Yes. Real doctor portraits, clinic interiors, and before-after assets will increase trust significantly after the client approves the project.

Why this color palette?

Warm white signals cleanliness, teal supports trust, sage softens dental anxiety, and gold gives the page a premium cosmetic-clinic feel.
